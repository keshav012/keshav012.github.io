About.

7+ years deep in the Salesforce ecosystem, building solutions that move the needle for enterprises across healthcare, field operations, and customer experience.

My focus is Salesforce Field Service — the messy, mission-critical slice of Salesforce where dispatchers, mobile workers, scheduling engines, and real-world assets all need to behave as one system. I architect end-to-end: from data model and territory design to mobile flows, work-order automation, and the integrations that make it all real.

Beyond Field Service, I lead delivery on Health Cloud, OmniStudio (Vlocity), Conga, and complex Salesforce integrations. I mentor developers, run code reviews, and obsess over security, scalability, and the developer experience of the orgs I leave behind.
